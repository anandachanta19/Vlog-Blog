About Me

This is what I do.

I’m Anand Achanta, a Python enthusiast and a Computer Science Engineering (CSE) undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Guwahati. Python has been my programming language of choice since I embarked on my coding journey. Its simplicity, readability, and versatility have always fascinated me. Whether it’s web development, data analysis, or automation scripts, Python is my go-to tool.

In the realm of web development, I specialize in building web applications using the Flask framework. Crafting seamless user experiences and robust backends is my forte. From REST APIs to dynamic web pages, I enjoy every aspect of web development. PostgreSQL is my trusted companion when it comes to databases. Designing efficient schemas, optimizing queries, and ensuring data integrity are challenges I embrace.

As a CSE undergraduate, I’m constantly learning and exploring new technologies. Whether it’s attending workshops, participating in hackathons, or diving into online courses, I thrive on expanding my knowledge. Beyond code, I’m a curious learner, a creative coder, and an open-source advocate. Let’s connect—I’m always up for a chat about tech, coding, or anything under the digital sun!
